Twistjam, developed from June - December 2015

Twistjam is a guitar learning app that allow guitar fellows to make and share guitar chords. Song developed along with three friends after work. Song led the iOS development and rails backend. The app is written entirely in swift.

Used Core Animation to animate chords falling down and disappearing


We create a custom UICollectionView that resembles a guitar fretboard to add and edit chords.


All user-created information are saved in Core Data and transmitted between our heroku server using Alamofire.

LifeSource Gallery of Hope, developed October 2015

LifeSource Gallery of Hope is an iPad app Song developed at Software For Good for a local organization LifeSource that shows organ donors and awaiting transplants.

It features a custom UICollection like the Pinterest app with image fitting to their height in 3 columns.


mAGIC

mAGIC is an app Song co-developed at Software For Good for health department of University of Minnesota, it is a 7-day questionnaire app for ovary cancer prone people to seek for genetic couselling. Song and his coworker engineer envisioned the flower-roots look of the app and held weekly meetings with the health group to discuss features and improvements.

Notification, notifies people every morning on where they left off last night to continue the survey.
